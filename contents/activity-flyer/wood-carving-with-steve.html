    Wood Carving with Steve

    Small wood, big charm: join Steve Heveron in Hall C for relaxed carving sessions—think custom pens you actually want to use, yard birds with personality, and clever little pieces you finish before dinner. Not a sawdust factory or a giant remodel—just sharp tools, good coaching, and neighbors who get a kick out of making something real.

    About this activity

    Steve focuses on small-scale projects with room for wit and skill—giftable pens, folk-art birds for the flower bed, and other tidy carvings you can start and finish without renting a forklift. First-timers who are curious about the tools? Welcome. Folks who already speak “grain direction” and just want company? Also welcome. The vibe is neighborly: trade a tip, borrow an idea, and leave with shavings on your shoes and a grin.

    Chair: Steve Heveron. Catch him on a Tuesday in Hall C, or email the event coordinator for roster contact info.
