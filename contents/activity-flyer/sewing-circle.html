    Sewing Circle

    Bring your machine or hand work to Hall C for a friendly Sewing Circle—repairs, quilts, or dreams pinned together one afternoon at a time. Swap tricks, borrow an opinion on a seam, and enjoy the company. Season: from November 5 through the last Thursday in March (see calendar for the final date).
