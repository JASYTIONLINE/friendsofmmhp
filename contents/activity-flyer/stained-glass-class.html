    Stained Glass Class

    Turn a sheet of glass into a story: Stained Glass Class is part skill-building, part art studio—cut lines that curve just right, learn how the pieces come together, and watch color and light do the show-off part. It’s the fun of picking up something new while you build beautiful projects you’ll actually hang, gift, or brag about—no pressure, lots of neighborly “look what I did” energy.

    About this activity

    Whether you’ve never scored a pane or you’re polishing old habits, this class is about learning together: patterns, patience, and the small victories that make glass look like magic. Expect encouragement, practical tips, and the satisfaction of a finished piece that catches the sun and stops visitors mid-sentence.

    Chair: Steve Heveron. Say hello on a Monday or Thursday in Hall C, or email the event coordinator for roster contact info.
