    Winter Ice Cream Social

    Cold treats, warm hellos: on the third Sunday of every month we scoop a flavor of the week—vanilla, orange sherbet, root-beer floats, or whatever's next—while neighbors trade stories and kids debate sprinkles vs. no sprinkles. Free, quick (half an hour), and open to the whole park.

    About this activity

    This one's simple: you're invited. The Winter Ice Cream Social meets on the third Sunday of each month during the season—for every resident who likes a little sweetness and a lot of hello. Grab a bowl or cone, try the flavor of the week, and meet members from around the park: creamy vanilla, citrusy orange sherbet, an old-school root-beer float bar, or a curveball Sandy dreams up. No charge. Bring the kids, bring a friend, or come solo and leave with new names to wave at on golf carts.

    Chair and contact is Sandy Steenblock. Questions? Ask at the hall on a Sunday, or email the event coordinator for roster contact info.
