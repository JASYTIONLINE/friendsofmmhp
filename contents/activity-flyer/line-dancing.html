    Seasonal activity

    Line Dancing

    Come try line dancing in Hall B—it is easier than it looks, and you do not need a partner. We work on crowd-pleasing routines you will see everywhere: think Electric Slide, Cupid Shuffle, Tush Push, Cowboy Boogie, maybe a simple waltz-line—each broken down so first-timers can follow the first hour. Many neighbors say they learn the pattern in one morning and still recognize it that same night when the music comes on at a park social.
