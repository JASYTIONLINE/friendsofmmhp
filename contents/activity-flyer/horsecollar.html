    About this activity

    Fair warning: neighbors describe Horsecollar as a knock-down, drag-out bar fight that forgot to invite the actual flying bottles—same adrenaline, bar shuffleboard's rulebook, teams trading rounds while the aggregate score keeps score of every grudge. It's loud-in-spirit, soft on furniture, brutal only to the team that didn't practice their line. Winner gets bragging rights; loser starts the group text about next Sunday.

    New to it? Bring curiosity and a thick skin for gentle ribbing. Chair and contact is Bob Rosati—he knows the rules, the legends, and who still owes the group a comeback story. Catch him at the courts or ask the event coordinator for roster contact info.
