    Computer Class

    Computer Class is a relaxed tour of the PC world—no pop quiz, no geek shaming. Explore new ideas, get comfortable with today’s technology, and trade “how do I…?” questions with neighbors who’ve been there. You might practice a handy shortcut, demystify an update, or learn why your cell phone has seventeen ways to silence a group chat—and pick the one you actually want.

    About this activity

    Whether you’re curious about Windows basics, staying safer online, or just want someone patient to explain what “the cloud” really means this week, this class is about familiarizing yourself with the tools we all live with—desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone. Bring your questions; we learn faster when we laugh at the same confusing dialog box together.

    Chair: John Viestenz (Computer Club). Stop by Hall C on a Tuesday morning, or email the event coordinator for roster contact info.
