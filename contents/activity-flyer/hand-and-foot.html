    Hand & Foot

    Not wheels—cards. Hand & Foot is the partnership game where you burn through a pile called your hand, then dig into your foot, building melds while the discard pile dares you to pick it up. Think canasta’s Southern-drawl storytelling cousin: lots of decks, big books on the table, and room for newcomers who just need one patient partner to explain the rhythm.

    About this activity

    Hand & Foot belongs to the same happy family as canasta and classic rummy: draw, meld, manage wild cards, and time your “go out” so your team’s score sings. Rules vary slightly by table—local veterans will tell you how this group likes to play—but the heart of it is always the same: teamwork, card-table banter, and one more round before you call it a night.

    Never played? Say so when you walk in; someone will get you sorted. Email the event coordinator for contact info for table hosts or schedule questions.
