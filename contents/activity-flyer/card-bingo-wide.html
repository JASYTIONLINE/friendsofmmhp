    Card Bingo Night

    Looking for something fast, fun, and a little competitive? Card Bingo brings people together around the table for easy-to-learn play and real cash pots.

    About this activity

    Card Bingo is a high-energy, easy-to-learn game that brings people together around the table. With simple rules, low buy-in, and real cash pots, every round is exciting. Whether you are here to win or just enjoy the atmosphere, the more tables playing, the bigger the payouts get.

    Here is how it works: each table has 4 players using 1 deck of cards. Every player is dealt 13 cards. The dealer flips one card at a time, and if you have that card, you discard it. The goal is simple: be the first to empty your hand.

    There are 13 rounds total: 4 nickel games, 4 dime games, 4 quarter games, and 1 dollar game. Each player buys in with a total of $2.60 ($1 bill, 4 quarters, 4 dimes, 4 nickels). The winner of each round takes the entire pot from all tables.

    Bring your change, grab a seat, and get ready to play. The more players and tables in the game, the bigger the pot and the better the energy. Do not miss your chance to shout BINGO, clear your hand, and take it all.
