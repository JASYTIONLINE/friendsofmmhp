    Summer Ice Cream Social

    When the Winter Ice Cream Social is on hiatus, this one carries the cone—same slot, same spirit: the third Sunday of every month (March through mid-November), 1:00–1:30 PM, a flavor of the week—vanilla, orange sherbet, root-beer floats, or whatever Sandy lines up—and the whole park invited for a free, quick scoop of fun.

    About this activity

    Identical rhythm to the Winter Ice Cream Social, opposite season on the calendar: third Sunday monthly while the park is outside the Winter social's dates. No charge. Bring kids, bring neighbors, sample the flavor of the week—then get back to your Sunday with sticky fingers and a fuller hello list.

    Chair and contact is Sandy Steenblock. Questions? Ask at the hall on a meet-up Sunday, or email the event coordinator for roster contact info.
