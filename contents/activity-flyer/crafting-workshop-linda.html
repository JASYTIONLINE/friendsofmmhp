    Seasonal activity

    Crafting Workshop with Linda

    You are invited to pull up a chair in Hall C and spend a relaxed Friday afternoon on arts and crafts with neighbors. Linda hosts a workshop where the projects can change from week to week—papers, fabrics, simple décor, seasonal ideas, or whatever the group is excited to try—so you can jump in, learn a little, and take home something you made.

    About this activity

    No studio pedigree required—just curiosity and a willingness to cut, fold, glue, or stitch alongside friends. Bring a project from home if you have one, or show up empty-handed and use what the table offers that day.

    Host: Linda Jensen. For questions or to offer a project idea, stop by on a Friday or email the event coordinator for contact info.
