    Monthly meeting

    Bylaws Committee

    The McAllen Mobile Home Park Association holds its regular Wednesday association meeting at 9:00 AM on the first Wednesday of each month. The Bylaws Committee meets immediately after that session ends. This is a serious working session—please treat it accordingly. Season: November 4 through March 3 (e.g. Nov 4, 2026–Mar 3, 2027).
