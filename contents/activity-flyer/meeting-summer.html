    Association Meeting — Summer

    Weekly meeting of the McAllen Mobile Home Park Association. Residents discuss current events, hear updates on upcoming activities, deconflict schedules, and work through park business together. This is a chance for all park members to stay informed and be part of the solution. Summer season: April 1 through November 3 each year; the winter association meeting listing takes over November 4.
