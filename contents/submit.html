    Submit an Event

    Fill in every field below. The page loads your current calendar data to suggest the next feature id and defaults. When you submit, your device opens Share or your email app with a CSV of the event row plus your photos attached. On desktops that cannot attach files automatically, a ZIP is downloaded—attach it (or the CSV and images inside) to the email that opens for the event coordinator.

    • Featured card title — main text on the featured card. Max 48 characters.
    • Featured card category — category or subtitle (for example Dance, Karaoke). Max 48 characters.
    • The date line on the card is set automatically from the event date you choose (no separate field).

    Event details

    Pick the day of your event—not today’s date unless the event really is today.

    This is when the event starts. Use 24-hour time (for example 19:00 for 7:00 p.m.).

    When the event ends. If there is no specific ending time, choose Until we get tired (first in the list). Otherwise pick a time.

    Choose from the hall list. If you pick Other, the Other location field opens up directly below—fill it in so the coordinator knows where to send people.

    This box stays read-only until you choose Other under Location above. Then type where the event will actually be held.

    The everyday title of the event—the words you would say out loud (for example “Tuesday Game Night”).

    A short kind of event in your own words—card games, a dance, a dinner, karaoke, and so on.

    A short description for the coordinator: what will happen, any fee, whether people should bring food, if food will be provided, and anything else that should appear on the flyer or detail page.

    Active listing

    Leave checked so the event shows once the coordinator adds it to the calendar data. Uncheck only if this submission should stay hidden.

    Featured placement

    Check if you want this night considered for featured spots when the site’s rules allow it.

    Images for the event coordinator

    Featured image is required. Additional photos are optional. Where your device supports it, images are included when you share or email the event coordinator.

    Email size (Gmail & Outlook): Keep each photo under about 4 MB and all photos together under about 16 MB so the message is less likely to be blocked or rejected. Larger files may still be sent, but delivery can fail—we’ll warn you if your selection exceeds these guides.
