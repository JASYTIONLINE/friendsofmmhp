# Presentation (outline)

Use the **simplest format** your instructor allows (slide deck, recorded walkthrough, or both).

## Story arc (problem → result)

1. **Problem / audience** — Residents need one trustworthy place for park events; coordinator needs structured handoff.
2. **Product** — Quick live demo or screenshots: home calendar, recurring rail, featured cards, submit flows.
3. **Architecture** — Static HTML/CSS/JS, `mmhp-master-data.json`, no custom server; Google Calendar embed alongside curated JSON.
4. **Decisions** — One or two trade-offs (e.g. static + coordinator vs. full CMS).
5. **Iteration** — What changed after Project 01 or QA.
6. **Results & lessons** — What shipped; what you’d improve next.
